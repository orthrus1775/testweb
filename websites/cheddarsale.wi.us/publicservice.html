DeForest Public Services

Welcome to the Village of Cheddarsale's Public Service Department – the backbone of our community's infrastructure and sustainability efforts. As stewards of public services, our department plays a vital role in ensuring the safety, cleanliness, and functionality of our village. We are here to serve you with integrity, accountability, and excellence. Explore our department page to learn more about our various services, initiatives, and how we're working together to build a brighter future for the Village of Cheddarsale.
