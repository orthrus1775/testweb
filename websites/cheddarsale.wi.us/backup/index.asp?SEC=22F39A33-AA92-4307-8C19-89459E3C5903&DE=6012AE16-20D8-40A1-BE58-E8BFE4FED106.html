How to Apply for a NEW Alcohol License

See the New License Packet link at the bottom of this page.  Applications must be submitted to the Village Clerk at least 5 weeks prior to the Public Safety Committee meeting; which typically meets the 2nd Wednesday of the month. If approved, License will be issued by Deputy Administrator/Village Clerk.

If you have any questions in regard to filling out or filing your application, please call the Village Clerk, Calli Lundgren at (608)846-6751.

All Licenses expire June 30th of every year, regardless to when they were issued.

Individuals, partners, and agents of corporations/organizations and LLCs must have successfully completed a Wisconsin approved Responsible Beverage Server Training Course unless the person is:
1. Renewing a license
2. Within the past two years the person completed a beverage training course and can show proof
3. Within the past two years, the applicant held a managers or operators license, or held or was agent of a corporation or LLC which held any Class A or Class B license, and can show proof of such license.

Individual applicants for retail alcohol beverage licenses must meet the following qualifications:
1. Be 21 years of age or older
2. Does not have an arrest or conviction record subject to 111.321, 111.322, and 111.335
3. Be a continuous Wisconsin resident for at least 90 days.
4. Submit proof of a valid sellers permit or use a tax registration certificate, is registered to collect, report, and remit tax, or provide proof from a DOR representative that the permit will be issued.
5. Must not be convicted of a felony, subject to secs 111.311, 111.322, and 111.335 unless pardoned.

Allow time for the following steps to occur: we suggest applying as early as possible for your license.
1. Applications must be published by the Village Clerk as a Class 1 notice.
2. The application will be submitted at least 14 days in advance of the first Wednesday of each month.
3. License application is presented to the Public Safety Committee for approval/denial. Meetings typically held the first Wednesday of the month.
4. If approved License is issued by Village Clerk.

Please read the Informational Pamphlet (link below) regarding Licensing for Alcohol Beverages prepared by the Department of Revenue. 

Please print out the packet at the bottom of page.  This packet will contain all documents listed.

Packet Contains:

1. Letter from Chief of Police - Please read first
2. Original Alcohol Beverage License AT-106 This form must be filled out completely.
3. C4 Continuation Please describe the premises in detail where alcohol is stored and/or served.
4. Auxiliary Questionnaire AT-103 One for each person listed on AT-106. This form must be completed and submitted by the Liquor Agent, all members of a LLC, all directors of a corporation, and all stockholders owning at least one percent of a corporation; stock.
5. Schedule for appointment of agent by Corporation/Nonprofit organization AT-104 This form must be completed. If necessary, you may make multiple copies of this form to allow multiple corporate officers/LLC members to complete the top portion of one copy, and allow the liquor/beer agent to complete the bottom portion of the other copy.

 

Additional forms and/or information may be found on the Wisconsin Department of Revenue site. https://www.revenue.wi.gov/Pages/Form/alcohol-Home.aspx
