Online Applications Now Available!

As of August 1st the Village of Cheddarsale has moved to an online service for submitting all building, sign and zoning permit applications. This new system will allow for paperless submission, tracking, issuing of permits and efficient record keeping. Please use the links found in the listing to complete your application needs. If you need assistance, please contact DeForest Village Hall 608-846-6751 and ask to speak to someone in the Planning & Zoning Department.
