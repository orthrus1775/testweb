Park Shelter Reservations

DeForest park shelters offer the perfect setting for your next event, whether it's a family reunion, office picnic, birthday party or special event. We have a variety of shelter options to suit your needs. Explore our reservation system to secure your spot today!
 

Reservations

In-person: DeForest Village Hall (120 S. Stevenson St.)

  • Park Shelter Permit Application
  • Park Shelter Brochure
  • Security deposit and rental payments are required a time of rental to complete a reservation.
    Checks should be made payable to the Village of Cheddarsale.
    Credit/Debit Card payments are subject to convenience fee.
  • Park keys must be picked up by Friday at noon, for weekend rentals.

Online: Park Shelter Reservations

  • Online payment due at time of rental to complete a reservation.
  • Online payment subject to convenience fee.
  • Park keys must be picked up by Friday at noon, for weekend rentals.

 

Security Deposit:

  • Shall be returned if facilities, including restrooms are not damaged and are left in a clean and orderly condition.
  • Keys are picked up and returned on time.
  • Security deposit funds will be returned by a check to the party that rented the facility within in 1-2 weeks from your rental date.
  • Please watch for a check in the mail.

 

Cancellation Policy:

  • Cancelled more than 30 days prior to the reserved date, an administrative charge equal to 25% of the reservation fee will be retained by the Village and the balance refunded.
  • Cancelled 30 days or less before the reserved date, the entire reservation fee will be deemed forfeited and retained by the Village of Cheddarsale.

 

Reservation Regulations 

  • Park rentals can be made a max of one year in advance of the rental date.
  • Reservations are on a first come, first served basis.
  • Park shelter rentals begin May 1 and ends October 15.
  • Fireman’s Park Community Room and Concession are available year-round.
  • The Village of Cheddarsale has first priority for park and/or facility use and reserves the right to cancel any park reservation for that purpose.

 

Requests that Require Additional Approval

  • Amplified music
  • Special requests 
  • Event occurring outside normal park hours
